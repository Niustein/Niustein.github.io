Kin'Garoth

TL;DR

  • Don't be in front of the tank who has Kin'gorath's threat
  • Tanks swap at 2 stacks
  • Dodge Ruiner
  • Move out of the way of Diabolic Bomb as it circles the arena
  • Save cooldowns for add phases
  • Soak annihilation
  • Leave group with Decimation
  • Stack in Demolish
  • Recommended Lust/Hero timings: During an add phase, which one doesn't really matter
  • ?
  • Loot

Boss Abilities:

-Forging StrikeStacking DoT on Tank which causes them to take 200% more damage from Forging Strikes. Nobody should be in front of the boss when this is cast as it cleaves off the tank
     Tanks should swap on 2 stacks

-Reverberating StrikeSmashes a target in melee range dealing some damage to anyone in a 5 yard radius. This leaves behind a permanent pylon that explodes for damage any time Reverberating Strike is cast
     Avoid being stacked in melee range or being on a pylon when it goes off again

-Ruinerwill be cast at 100 energy. Pointing at a random target and a few seconds later expelling a beam of fel energy, dealing a massive amount of damage and slowly rotates either clockwise or counterclockwise
     Everyone needs to do everything they can to avoid the beam, as it will kill anyone in a very short amount of time

-Diabolic Bombwill create a bomb (3 in heroic) that rolls around the arena. Triggering the bomb will deal raid wide damage, and less damage the further away you are.
     Players should make sure not to get hit by the bomb, and allow the tanks to explode the bombs in the Add Phase

-Sever (Phase 2)Stacking DoT on the tank.
     Tanks should swap on 3 stacks (as soon as it falls off the other tank)

-Apocolypse ProtocolSignals the beginning of the Add Phase. Kin'gorath will put a protective barrier up reducing the damage he takes by 99%. Meanwhile, adds will spawn on branches off the main arena.
     All players should swap to the adds

-Flames of the Forgewill deal raid wide damage during the Add Phase. Make sure to stay by a healer during this phase.

-Apocalypse BlastDuring the Add Phase, giant embers will rain down. Players should avoid standing in the indicated circles

Add Phase:

-There are 3 different types of Adds. Demolisher, Decimator, Annihilator.

-When the Add phase starts, the adds will start with 30 stacks of Initializing where each stack increases the damage they take by 30 seconds. Every second each add loses one stack of Initializing. After 30 seconds, the add will join the fight and cast their ability which must be dealt with along with Kin'garoth. When an add is killed, it will deal 6% of Kin'Garoth's HP (4% on Heroic)

-On normal only 2 adds will spawn at a time, whereas on Heroic there will be 3 adds that spawn per add phase.
