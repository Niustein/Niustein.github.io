Portal Keeper Hasabel

TL;DR

  • Tanks swap on 3 stacks of Reality Tear
  • Tanks should tank boss on edge to give room on the platform for collapsing world
  • Kill adds from transport portal ASAP
  • Split raid into two, portal team and everyone else
  • Dodge Collapsing world and Felstorm Barrage
  • Recommended Lust/Hero timings: At 30% when the void portal opens
  • ?
  • Loot

Boss Abilities:

- Reality Tearputs a dot on the tank and upon expiring does a burst of damage to the entire raid
     Tanks should swap on 3 stacks

-Transport PortalSummons adds based on which phase of the fight it is that must be dealt with
     All DPS on the main platform should immediately swap to the adds and burst/cleave them down before resuming damage on the boss.

- Collasping Worldwill place a large circle originating from the boss that does a massive amount of damage to anyone standing within. Upon expiring, the raid takes some damage
     Tanks should be tanking the boss along the edge of the arena so that players have more room to maneuver out of the circle

- Felstorm Barragewill cross the arena with a large rectangular beam (3 beams on heroic) and will damage and knock back any players standing in the beam upon detonation.
     All players should make sure to avoid standing in the detonation zone. Especially important for the tank and any melee DPS as the knockback CAN knock players off of the arena

- Empower Xoroth (90%),Rancora (60%),Nazthreza (30%)activates the fire/poison/void portals at certain boss HP thresholds and summons an elite mob. While the elite mob is alive, the raid is taking high ticking damage from "Empowered-Portal-" and after the elite dies the dot becomes "Portal-" which still does ticking raid wide damage but significantly less
     The raid should be split into two and the portal team should go up to engage this elite mob. All three elite mobs can cast Unstable Portal which should be interrupted.
         Vulcanar (xoroth's portal) can also cast Flames of Xoroth deals moderate damage and applies a dot to all players within 10 yards.
         Lady Dacidion (rancora's portal) can cast posion essence and felsilk wrap. The first places a dot on all players on the platform which deals 10% of current health per second, and felsilk wrap deals damage a player and immobilizes them until the wrap is destroyed.
         Lord Eilgar (nathreza's portal) will cast Delusion's on a healer which reduces their healing and absorbs by 100% but gives them 2% mana back per second, should be dispelled when mana is not a concern. He will also cast Corrupt which hits all players within 20 yards and increases the damage they take by 15% for 4 seconds.
