Imonar The Soulhunter

TL;DR

  • P1+2: Ranged DPS/Healers stack
  • P3: everybody splits
  • P1: Tanks swap on 3/4 stacks of Shock Lance
  • P1: When Sleep Canister goes out, have that player move out before dispelling
  • P1: have tank reduce number of Pulse Gernades on the first platform
  • P2: Tanks swap on ~3 stacks of Sever
  • P2: Dodge Shrapnel Blast
  • P2: Move Charged Blast out of the way
  • Recommended Lust/Hero timings: Start of P3
  • ?
  • Loot

Boss Abilities:

-Shock Lance (P1)Stacking DoT on Tank which causes them to take more damage. In addition when shock lance goes out again for each DoT on the tank it will hit a random raid member
     Tanks should swap on 3/4 stacks (as soon as it falls off the other tank)

-Pulse Gernade (P1)Lobs a gernade that persist on the ground until triggered. Upon detonation, explodes dealing large damage to anyone in a 3 yard radius
     DPS should avoid hitting these by rotating as a unit. The tank not affected can hit a gernade to reduce the clutter on the platform, as this is the location for both phase 1 and phase 3

-Sleep Canister (P1)lobs an undodgeable canister at several players and put them in a slumber for 20 seconds. When this debuff expires or is dispelled, anyone within 7 yards will get slept for 8 seconds (and this debuff cannot be dispelled)
     The person targeted by Sleep Canister moves away from the group, healers make sure no one is near the target before dispelling them

-Infernal Rockets (All Phases)will target anyone who is not on the same platform as Imonar. Dealing a large amount of fire damage and increasing the amount of fire damage they take. Avoid this by staying on the same platform as Imonar

-Sever (Phase 2)Stacking DoT on the tank.
     Tanks should swap on 3 stacks (as soon as it falls off the other tank)

-Shrapnel Blast (P2)Similar to Pulse Gernade will cause zones on the ground which deal a large amount of damage and leave anyone in the area with a hard hitting DoT.
     All players should avoid these

-Charged Blast (P2)will target two players from ships around the arena and after 7 seconds will blast these players (and anyone in line with these players) knocking them back and leaving a dot which lasts for 30 seconds.
     If targetted by Charged Blast, move to the ends of the arena and make sure that there is no other player in line with the blast.

-Empowered Shock Lance (P3)Works similar to Shock Lance from P1, but is now permanent
     Tanks should swap every few stacks to keep the damage to the raid as low as possible for as long as possible

-Empowered Pulse Gernade (P3)instead of lobbing gernades at players, random players will be affected by empowerd pulse gernade and will pulse AoE damage from them.
     All players in phase 3 should be split to avoid taking un-necessary damage from empowered pulse gernade

-Empowered Shrapnel Blast (P3)Litters the area with green swirlies on the ground which deals a moderate amount of damage and puts a permanent dot on the player
     Players should move out of the swirlies and should never be hit by them as they cause a lot of damage to your healers

Transition Phases:

-At 66% and 33% Imonar will cross a bridge connecting the two platforms and begin channeling Conflagration and lose the Infernal Rockets ability. This ability hurls burning orbs down the platform which deal a massive amount of fire damage to any player that gets hit by it, as well as slowing them by 50% for 8 seconds. During the channel, Imonar takes 99% reduced damage. When this ability is interrupted, he regains Infernal Rockets.

-On the bridge there are many pulse gernades and statis traps that players must avoid. Although players should coordinate to hit a few a of the traps as these will remain on the bridge for the second intermission along with blastwires which deal raid wide fire damage when triggered.
