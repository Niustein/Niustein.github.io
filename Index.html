-The boss introduction picture on the top right of each guide page, as well as links to the abilities themselves, are taken from wowhead and FatbossTV.

    Terminology:
  • WoW = World of Warcraft
  • DPS = Damage per Second. Also Refers to a damage dealer's role
  • Taunt/Swap = When the tank takes the boss off the other tank by using an ability called Taunt
  • Normal/Heroic = Difficulties that are available for the raid
  • Raid = All of the bosses in the instance form a raid. Also used to indicate the players inside of the raid ("This ability hit the entire raid")
  • Hero/Lust = An ability that gives the entire raid a damage boost.
  • Interrupt = An ability to stop the target from casting their ability
  • P1/P2/... = Phase 1, Phase 2, etc.
