Aggramar

TL;DR

  • Tanks swap at 5-7 stacks of Taeshalach's Reach
  • Non tanks split up to Scorching Blaze
  • When Aggramar reaches 100 energy, all non-tanks stack behind aggramar
  • (Heroic Only) During intermission, keep embers CC'd and allow them to reach Aggarmar one at a time. Transition out of intermission ASAP. Dodging Meteor Swarm
  • For normal, kill all of the embers ASAP
  • Dodge flare+wake of flame during P1+P2+P3
  • Recommended Lust/Hero timings: During the second intermission phase (Heroic). On Normal, on the pull.
  • ?
  • Loot

Boss Abilities:

-Taeshalach's Reach. Whenever Aggramar melees, the nearest target is also hit, if nobody is nearby the primary target gets hit twice. This also applies s stacking debuff that increases the physical damage taken by the main target by 5% for 12 seconds.
     Tanks should swap on 5-7 stacks of this debuff

-Taeshalach TechniqueAggramar builds up energy. When he reaches 100 energy, he will use this technique which consists of For Breaker -> Flame Rend -> Foe Breaker -> Flame Rend -> Searing Tempest
     Upon Aggramar reaching 100 energy. All non-tanks stack up behind Aggramar. The tanks will swap who takes foe breaker and then point flame rend at the rest of the raid

-Foe Breaker. Deals a massive amount of physical damage to all targets in front of Aggramar. In addition anyone hit gets a debuff that increases Foe Breakers damage by 500% for 30 seconds.
     Tanks each take a foe breaker during Taeshalach Technique as taking a second one is instant death.

-Flame Rend. Does a MASSIVE amount of damage split evenly amongst everyone hit. This also knocks players backwards. If not enough players are hit by Flame-Rend. Aggramar will get a permanent 50% stacking damage buff.
     Players should be directly behind Aggramar

-Searing TempestAfter the second Flame Rend, Aggramar will cast Searing Tempest which is a giant circle originating from his position that will explode after several seconds. Anyone caught in the blast takes a large amount of damage and is also disoriented for 5 seconds.      After flame rend, players should run out of the circle

-Scorching Blaze. Random players get ignited and deal pulsing AoE damage to anyone within 4 yards of them.
     All non-tanks should be split 4 yards to reduce the amount of overlap and damage taken during Scorching Blaze

-Wake of FlameIn P1: Will originate from Aggramar and travel outwards, anyone getting hit will take a moderate amount of damage and also have lingering DoT for 12 seconds. In P3, Aggramar will stop manually casting it, but it will be attached to Flare.

-Flare. In P2 and P3, Aggramar marks up to 3 locations that explode after 4 seconds.

-Corrupt Aegis. At 80% and 40%. Aggramar will go into an Add phase, putting up Corrupt Aegis and reducing the damage he takes by 99%. -Blazing Eruption. When an ember during the add phase reaches Aggramar, the entire raid gets a stack of Blazing Eruption which is a ticking DoT that does damage for 20 seconds. It also increases the damage players take from Blazing Eruption by 100%.
     Multiple applications of blazing eruption is almost certainly a wipe as the damage output that goes through becomes too high.

-Meteor Swarm. During the add phase, giant orange circles will appear on the ground that deals moderate damage to anyone caught in the area.

Add Phase:

-At 80% and 40% marks the add phases for Aggramar

-When the Add phase starts, Aggramar will go to the cetner of the room and apply Corrupt Aegis making him virtually un-damagable. 8 adds will spawn, 6 embers and 2 flames. When the flames are killed the transition will occur. On normal, kill the 6 embers ASAP. On heric, the embers must be led into Aggramar one at a time. 2 ember DoT applications will result in a wipe. And if the embers are not dealt with in a reasonable amount of time, they will explode applying the debuff for each remaining ember.
